  Expectancy tells you how much you can expect to make (or lose)
  on every single trade on average. It is the mathematical edge
  of the strategy.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  EXPECTANCY MODEL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Win Rate:                     35.02%
    Percentage of trades that hit take profit.

  Average Win (R-Multiple):     2.5000R
    On average, each winning trade returns 2.50 times
    the amount risked.

  Average Loss (R-Multiple):    1.0000R
    On average, each losing trade loses 1.00 times
    the amount risked.

  Payoff Ratio:                 2.5000
    Average Win divided by Average Loss in R terms.

  Breakeven Win Rate:           28.57%
    The minimum win rate needed to break even given this
    payoff ratio. Any win rate above this means the strategy
    has a positive edge.

  Edge Over Breakeven:          +6.45%
    How much the actual win rate exceeds the breakeven point.
    This is the strategy's mathematical advantage.

  Expectancy per Trade (R):     +0.2257R
    On average, each trade is expected to return 0.2257
    times the amount risked.

  Expectancy per Trade ($):     $+633.26
    The average dollar profit or loss per trade.
